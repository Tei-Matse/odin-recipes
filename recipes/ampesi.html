Ampesi Recipe

Description

This is a local Ghanaian recipe that consist of cooked Yam and Cocoyam leaves stew.

It is highly nutritious and very much enjoyed by people in Ghana.

Ingredients

  • Yam
  • Palm Oil
  • Cocoyam Leaves
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Pepper
  • Boiled Eggs
  • Salt
  • Tilapia Fish

Steps

  1. Peel the Yam and slice into appropriate pieces.
  2. Thoroughly wash the sliced yam and boil for 30 minutes.
  3. Wash the Cocoyam leaves and boil, afterwards blend it.
  4. Pour palm oil into a bowl and place on fire.
  5. Grind the tomatoes and pepper and add to the palm oil.
  6. Add the onions to the stew, mix and keep on fire for 10 minutes with intemittent stirring.
  7. Add the blended cocoyam leaves, add very little water and salt.
  8. Add the Tilapia fish and keep on fire for 5 minutes.
  9. Finally, peel the boiled eggs and add to the stew.
