Jollof Recipe

Description

This is a recipe called Jollof, which is a combination of rice and tomatoe stew prepared in a bowl together. The stew is prepared first, then the rice is added to it and stirred continuously until the stew is uniformly distributed on the rice.

The pasta is boiled separately and added to the Jollof and continuously stirred

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Pasta
  • Cooking Oil
  • Meat
  • Pepper
  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • Salt
  • Maggie Cube Seasoner
