Jollof Recipe

Description

This is a recipe called Jollof, which is a combination of rice and tomatoe stew prepared in a bowl together. The stew is prepared first, then the rice is added to it and stirred continuously until the stew is uniformly distributed on the rice.

The pasta is boiled separately and added to the Jollof and continuously stirred

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Pasta
  • Cooking Oil
  • Meat
  • Pepper
  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • Salt
  • Maggie Cube Seasoner

Steps

  1. Slice the tomatoes, onions, meat, and ginger into pieces.
  2. Put the cooking oil on fire and add the sliced tomaotoes and onions, and stir.
  3. Grind the pepper and sliced ginger and add to the stew base.
  4. Let it boil for 10 minutes and add the rice to the stew and mix thoroughly until the stew is evenly spread on the rice.
  5. Add sufficient water to the rice, add sufficient salt, cover it and allow it to cook for 45 minutes, with intermittent stirring
  6. Boil the Pasta for 20 minutes.
  7. Fry the sliced meat in cooking oil until it becomes golden brown.
  8. Add the boiled pasta and fried meat to the Jollof and stir.
  9. Add your preferred maggie cube seasoning.
