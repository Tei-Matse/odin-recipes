Banku Recipe

Description

This is a recipe called Banku and Okro Soup. The banku is prepared from corn that has been ground.

The soup is prepared separately. This can be eaten with crab, fish or beef meat.

Ingredients

  • Corn dough
  • Cassava dough
  • Okro
  • Crab
  • Salmon fish
  • Beef meat
  • Palm oil
  • Tomoatoes
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Mix the corn dough with the cassava dough.
  2. Add water to the mix and mix thoroughly until and even mass is formed. Ensure there are no lumps formed.
  3. Put water in a bowl and add the mix and place on fire.
  4. With the help of a driving stick, drive the mixture continuously until an even mass of semi-solid food is formed.
  5. Add water to the mixture and sufficient quantity of salt and keep on driving.
  6. Prepare the okro soup by chopping the okro into pieces with the help of a grading equipment.
  7. Slice onions, tomatoes into pieces.
  8. Pour Palm oil into a bowl and bring to fire to boil.
  9. Add the sliced onions and tomatoes and stir.
  10. Add the crab, beef meat and salmon fish.
  11. Add the grated okro to the mixture and stir.
  12. Add a lot of water to the mixture and add salt as well.
  13. Keep it on fire until well cooked.
